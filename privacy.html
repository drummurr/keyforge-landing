Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 31, 2026

The short version: KeyForge is designed with privacy in mind. We don't collect, store, or share your personal data. Your practice sessions stay on your device.

Introduction

KeyForge ("we", "our", or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle information when you use our iOS application.

Information We Don't Collect

KeyForge is designed to work entirely on your device. We do not collect:

  • Personal identification information (name, email, etc.)
  • Practice session data or recordings
  • MIDI input or performance data
  • Location data
  • Device identifiers for tracking purposes
  • Usage analytics or behavioral data

Data Storage

All app data, including your saved exercises, practice sessions, and preferences, is stored locally on your device. This data is not transmitted to any external servers.

If you delete the app, all locally stored data will be removed from your device.

MIDI Keyboard Access

KeyForge may request permission to access connected MIDI devices. This access is used solely to receive your keyboard input for practice feedback. MIDI data is processed in real-time on your device and is never stored, transmitted, or shared.

Microphone Access

KeyForge does not require or request microphone access. All audio playback is output-only through the built-in synthesizer.

Subscriptions and Payments

Subscriptions are processed entirely through Apple's App Store. We do not have access to your payment information, credit card details, or Apple ID. All billing is handled by Apple according to their privacy policy.

Third-Party Services

KeyForge does not integrate with any third-party analytics, advertising, or tracking services. We do not share any data with third parties.

Children's Privacy

KeyForge does not knowingly collect any information from children under 13. Since we don't collect personal information from any users, there is no risk of children's data being collected.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected on this page with an updated revision date. We encourage you to review this policy periodically.

Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us at:

support@keyforgeapp.com

Your Rights

Since we don't collect personal data, there is no personal data to access, correct, or delete. Your practice data remains entirely under your control on your device.
